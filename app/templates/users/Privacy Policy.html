ICUConnect Privacy Policy

Effective: July 21st, 2021

Introduction

ICUConnect (“we”, “us”, or “our”) is a digital platform designed to help public hospitals in Kenya coordinate ICU bed availability, streamline patient referrals, and predict ICU occupancy using machine learning techniques. We are deeply committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we handle your information. This Privacy Policy outlines our data practices, including the information we collect, how we use it, your choices, and the steps we take to ensure your data remains secure and compliant with applicable laws.

This policy applies to all users of ICUConnect, including hospitals, hospital administrators, and hospital personnel (such as doctors and nurses).

About ICUConnect

ICUConnect is a web-based dashboard that enables authorized hospitals and their personnel to:

  • View and update ICU bed availability in real time.
  • Refer and transfer patients between hospitals.
  • Admit and discharge patients.
  • Receive notifications and alerts about ICU status.
  • Access predictive analytics for ICU occupancy.

The platform is currently designed and intended for research and demonstration purposes, with plans for future deployment in a real-world setting. Please note that some data, such as ICU bed capacities and occupancy, may be simulated for these purposes and should not be used for real-world clinical decision-making.

Information We Collect

The information we collect depends on your role and how you interact with ICUConnect.

1. Information You Provide

Account Registration

To use ICUConnect, hospital personnel and administrators must create an account. During registration, we collect your name, email address, hospital affiliation, and a password (securely hashed). Hospitals provide facility information, including name, location (latitude and longitude), the levels (level 1 to 6 according to the Kenyan health system classification), and a timezone.

Profile and Settings

You may provide additional information, such as your specific role (doctor, nurse, admin), and customize your notification preferences (sound settings, referral delay time, and in-app notifications).

Communications

If you contact us for support, feedback, or inquiries, we collect the information you provide, including your contact details and the content of your message, to personalize our assistance and address your needs effectively.

2. Information We Collect Automatically

Usage and Log Data

We automatically collect information about your activity on ICUConnect, such as login, verification times, actions performed (e.g., updating bed status, initiating or responding to referrals), and system settings. This data helps us improve the platform’s functionality, monitor performance, detect potential security threats, and maintain a detailed audit trail in line with privacy best practices and the Kenya Digital Act, 2019.

Notifications and Alerts

We log when notifications are sent and received, and any user interactions with these notifications, as well as your preferences for receiving them.

3. Information from Third Parties

Hospital Registry Data

Hospital facility information is sourced from the Kenya Master Health Facility Registry (KMHFR), the official national registry of health facilities in Kenya.

Simulated Data

As mentioned, some ICU bed capacity and occupancy data may be simulated for research and demonstration purposes during the platform's development and testing phases.

4. Indirect Collection of Patient Data

ICUConnect does not collect patient data directly from patients. Instead, patient information, which is categorized as sensitive personal data under Kenyan law, is entered into the system by authorized hospital personnel as part of routine hospital operations. This includes data related to patient admissions, discharges, referrals, and transfers. The information collected may include, but is not limited to:

  • Admission and discharge dates
  • Referrals and transfer details (e.g., referring hospital, receiving hospital, reason for transfer)
  • Patient demographic details such as name (masked on visualization), age and gender
  • ICU bed assignment and occupancy status

Responsibility for Patient Consent

The hospital, as the primary data controller, is responsible for obtaining appropriate and informed consent from patients for the collection and processing of their health data for the purposes of coordinating ICU resources, managing patient flow, and supporting hospital operations, as required by the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019, and the Digital Health Act, 2023. ICUConnect acts as a data processor (or joint controller, depending on the specific agreement with the hospital) on behalf of the hospitals, facilitating the lawful processing of this data.

This patient data is solely used to coordinate ICU resources, manage patient flow, and support hospital operations. On visualization within the platform, patient names are masked to protect privacy. Any patient-related data is strictly accessible only to authorized users within the specific hospital network involved in the patient's care and is not shared with third parties, except as required by law or for aggregated, anonymized research and reporting purposes.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following specific and legitimate purposes:

  • To operate and maintain the ICUConnect platform: This includes hosting, debugging, and improving the platform's core functionalities.
  • User Authentication and Access Management: To authenticate users and manage access based on their assigned roles (e.g., hospital administrator, doctor, nurse).
  • Facilitate Healthcare Coordination: To coordinate ICU bed availability, manage patient admissions, discharges, referrals, and transfers efficiently.
  • Provide Real-time Notifications and Alerts: To keep users informed about ICU status changes and patient events.
  • Generate Predictive Analytics: To display ICU occupancy predictions using machine learning models to aid hospital planning.
  • Customize User Experience: To personalize your experience based on your settings and preferences.
  • System Monitoring and Security: To monitor system usage, ensure the security and integrity of the platform, and maintain detailed audit trails for compliance.
  • Customer Support: To respond to your inquiries and provide technical or operational support.
  • Compliance with Legal and Regulatory Requirements: To meet obligations under the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019, the Digital Health Act, 2023, and other applicable laws.

How ICUConnect Works for Different Users

Hospitals

Hospitals are registered entities on ICUConnect, each with a profile containing facility information and ICU bed capacity. Each hospital designates administrators and users (hospital personnel) responsible for operations within that facility.

Administrators

Hospital administrators have elevated privileges to manage their hospital accounts, approve new users, manage bed counts, and assign other administrators within their facility. The specific allocation of administrative roles depends on each hospital's internal rules and workflows. Administrators can also view audit logs to ensure compliance with internal and external policies.

Hospital Personnel (Users)

Doctors, nurses, and other authorized staff use ICUConnect to admit and discharge patients, initiate and respond to referrals, and update ICU bed status. Users can customize their notification preferences, view real-time updates, and access dashboards relevant to their specific roles.

Notifications and Settings

ICUConnect provides a range of notification options to keep you informed:

  • In-app Notifications: Real-time alerts for admissions, discharges, referrals, and escalation alerts for unresponded referrals.
  • Email Notifications: Updates such as verification links after sign-up, notifications when your account has been approved by administrators, or when a new user signs up to your hospital and requires approval. These are sent to your registered email address.
  • Customizable settings: You can adjust your notification preferences, including enabling/disabling sound, setting notification thresholds, and choosing which events trigger alerts.

All notification preferences can be managed directly in your account settings. Please note that some notifications are mandatory for system security, operational functionality, and compliance purposes.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

With Other Users Authorized Users within the ICUConnect Healthcare Network

Information such as hospital name, current bed availability, and high-level referral status is shared with authorized personnel across hospitals within the ICUConnect network to facilitate coordinated patient care. Detailed patient referral and transfer information, including patient age, gender, and diagnosis, is strictly accessible only to personnel at the referring and receiving hospitals directly involved in the patient's transfer.

With Third Parties

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes. We may share data with regulatory authorities or law enforcement if required by a legal obligation.

Data Security

We take your privacy and security very seriously by implementing technical and organizational measures to protect your privacy and the security of your data.

  • All passwords are securely hashed and never stored in plain text.
  • Access to sensitive data is strictly restricted based on user roles and privileges, ensuring that only authorized personnel can access relevant information.
  • Data is stored in a secure, managed database.
  • We conduct regular security audits and continuous monitoring to detect, prevent, and respond to unauthorized access, data breaches, and other security incidents.
  • We adhere to the principle of data minimization, collecting and processing only the data that is necessary for the stated purposes.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services. Audit logs and historical data may be retained for compliance, research, and security purposes. You may request deletion of your account or data, subject to legal and operational requirements.

Your Rights and Choices

Following the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019, you have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access and Correction: You have the right to request access to the personal information we hold about you and to request corrections or updates to inaccurate or incomplete data at any time.
  • Notification Preferences: You can customize how you receive notifications and alerts through your account settings.
  • Account Deletion: You may request the deletion of your account. Some data may be retained for compliance or research.
  • Right to Object: You have the right to object to the processing of your personal data under certain circumstances.
  • Support: If you have any questions or wish to exercise any of your rights, please contact us using the details below.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the updated policy on our platform and updating the "Effective" date at the top of this page. Your continued use of ICUConnect after such changes constitutes your acceptance of the new Privacy Policy.
